About Royalbro Sanitation Ltd

Founded in 2015, Royalbro Sanitation Ltd is dedicated to elevating the sanitation experience across Kenya and beyond. We transformed the portable toilet industry by bringing dignity, class, and luxury to what was once considered a basic necessity.

Our mission is simple: to provide world-class portable sanitation solutions that exceed expectations. Every unit we deploy reflects our commitment to hygiene excellence, royal treatment, and uncompromising quality standards.
